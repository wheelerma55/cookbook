S'mores Recipe

Ingredients:
1 large marshmallow
1 graham cracker
1 bar of chocolate

Directions:
1. Heat the marshmallow over an open fire
   until it begins to brown and melt.

2. Break the graham cracker in half.
   Sandwich the chocolate between the crackers
   and the hot marshmallow. Allow the marshmallow
   to cool a moment before eating.
